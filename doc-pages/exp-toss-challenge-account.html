Launched ‘Challenge Account’ for group challenge participants, achieving 300K+ MAU within one month (fastest in company history), 70%+ week‑1 retention, ultimately reducing company‑wide churn by 5%

Background

Social Squad was tasked with defending engagement among challenge cohorts that powered Toss Bank's social growth flywheel but showed weakening retention after challenge completion.

Problem

Challenge participants lacked a shared savings workflow once the event ended, leading to 35% churn within two weeks and limited opportunities to cross-sell high-value financial products.

Solution

Partnered with compliance, finance, and engineering to design 'Challenge Account'—a shared balance with auto-progress dashboards, accountability nudges, and host controls for incentives—shipped in six weeks via phased beta.

Analytics

Instrumented cohort dashboards in Looker tracking activation funnel, invitation acceptance, challenge completion, and downstream deposits; ran hold-out experiments to isolate retention lift versus seasonal effects.

Result

Reached 300K+ MAU in the first month (fastest feature to scale), sustained 70% week-one retention, and brought churn for challenge graduates down by 5% at the company level while increasing average balance by 18%.

Follow-up Actions

Rolled out milestone badges, auto-renewed challenges, and referral hooks tailored to high-performing hosts; embedded account insights into lifecycle CRM journeys to nurture repeat challenge launches.
