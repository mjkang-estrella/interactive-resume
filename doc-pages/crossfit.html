Built AI product analyzing CrossFit performance to deliver personalized recommendations.

Background

Weekend athletes in my CrossFit community wanted actionable feedback between coaching sessions. Wearables captured plenty of data, but coaches lacked time to interpret trends or spot injuries early.

Problem

Manual video reviews and spreadsheet logging didn’t scale—athletes missed PRs, and coaches could not personalize programming for remote members. We needed a faster, insight-driven loop.

Solution

Designed CrossFit Analyzer: a mobile-first companion that ingests Garmin/Whoop metrics, uses pose estimation on barbell videos, and routes findings through an LLM tuned on CrossFit coaching cues to surface recovery and skill recommendations.

Analytics & Iteration

Instrumented each release against session tagging accuracy, RPE deltas, and survey feedback. Added mobility nudges when recovery flags spiked and improved lift detection after labeling 400+ reps with coaches.

Result & Next Steps

Early pilot teams logged 25% higher training consistency and reported fewer overuse injuries. Expanding the dataset with partner gyms and exploring PT integrations to build a holistic performance + recovery playbook.
