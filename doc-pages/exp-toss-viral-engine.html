Viral Engine

Background

Referral performance plateaued because invites felt transactional; we needed an always-on engine that could ride social trends while reinforcing Toss Bank's trusted brand voice.

Problem

Legacy campaigns required manual launches and decayed quickly; engineering capacity was constrained, and compliance required rigorous messaging controls for any shareable asset.

Solution

Designed a modular viral platform with collectible cards, social proof streaks, and loss-aversion copy variants; automated triggers based on behavioral milestones and embedded dynamic CTA deep links.

Analytics & Iteration

Instrumented share funnels, tracked virality (K-factor) in Amplitude, and A/B tested value propositions weekly; partnered with compliance to pre-approve message templates for rapid experimentation.

Result
