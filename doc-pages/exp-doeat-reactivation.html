Designed and scaled a dessert‑giveaway reactivation program, boosted dormant‑user returns 3x, membership conversions 5x, and maintaining 2x higher long‑term retention compared to baseline

Background

After rapid expansion, dormant cohorts ballooned—especially light users who saw DoEat as a weekend treat; we needed a retention lever that felt delightful yet was margin conscious.

Problem

Blanket coupons eroded unit economics and failed to create habit; we required a targeted program that reintroduced users to the evolving menu while showcasing membership benefits.

Solution

Launched a surprise dessert giveaway triggered by churn probability scores, partnering with top-rated bakeries; bundled a free item with a membership trial and contextual storytelling in push/email.

Analytics & Iteration

Built an uplift model comparing treated vs. control cohorts, monitored basket size, and A/B tested reward thresholds; iterated packaging based on qualitative feedback about perceived value.

Result

Dormant user reactivations tripled, membership conversions jumped 5x among the cohort, and reactivated users retained at twice the baseline after 90 days while keeping contribution margin positive.

Follow-up Actions

Automated the churn scoring pipeline, expanded to savory “Chef’s Choice” drops, and aligned CS to capture anecdotes for future lifecycle storytelling.
