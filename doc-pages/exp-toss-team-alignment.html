Team Alignment

Background

Social Squad was an 18-person pod spanning product, design, engineering, data, CX, and brand; rapid headcount growth created misaligned priorities and ad-hoc delivery rituals.

Problem

Teams lacked a shared view of outcomesâ€”roadmaps drifted toward feature volume, quarterly reviews were retroactive, and psychological safety dipped in engagement pulses.

Solution

Introduced outcome-based OKRs, bi-weekly discovery reviews, and structured feedback cadences; implemented Miro-based planning templates and Jira dashboards to visualize progress vs. impact.

Iteration

Ran anonymous health checks each sprint, adjusted rituals per squad feedback, and codified decision logs so leadership could unblock escalations within 24 hours.

Result

Engagement survey showed 95% satisfaction, teammate retention improved to 100% through the next two quarters, and cycle time on high-priority experiments dropped by 22%.

Follow-up Actions

Scaled the operating cadence to adjacent squads, shared templates at the product guild, and baked the health-check survey into onboarding for new PMs and chapter leads.
