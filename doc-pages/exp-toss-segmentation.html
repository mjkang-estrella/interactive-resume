Customer Segmentation

Background

Toss Bank's Club and Partner segments were growing but lacked differentiated product journeysâ€”most members saw generic in-app surfaces despite distinct financial behaviors.

Problem

Engagement dropped after onboarding because high-intent couples, roommates, and clubs expected tailored workflows; we needed segmentation robust enough to power personalization and marketing automation.

Solution

Partnered with data science to mine 1M+ user events in Snowflake using SQL and Python; surfaced five durable persona clusters, then mapped each to bespoke dashboards, shared goals, and nudges in-app and CRM.

Analytics & Iteration

Implemented lookalike modelling to measure conversion lift, ran weekly pulse surveys per persona, and iterated on feature gating, copy, and rewards based on funnel drop-offs and sentiment trends.

Result

Persona-led experiences lifted NPS by 20 points and drove a 30% uptick in daily joint account openings while reducing support tickets about confusing shared finances by 35%.

Follow-up Actions

Documented the segmentation framework, automated weekly refreshes, and seeded the taxonomy into marketing automation and product analytics to keep future launches aligned to persona needs.
