Background

As the CPO team's product strategy intern, I supported Hakuna's 2021 planning cycle; leadership needed clarity on which user segments fueled retention to prioritize roadmap bets.

Problem

Existing dashboards lumped all users together, masking differences between casual viewers, gift senders, and aspiring creators; roadmap debates lacked grounded numbers on segment value.

Solution

Pulled 3M+ behavioral events from Amplitude, built cohort definitions using RFM-style clustering, and collaborated with designers to map friction points for high-potential segments across the journey.

Analytics

Analyzed retention, monetization, and feature adoption by segment; stress-tested findings with qualitative interviews and presented a prioritization matrix for CPO reviews.

Result

Insights informed roadmap focus on emerging creators and social watch parties, driving a 15% lift in core user conversion after aligned features shipped, and set the foundation for later metaverse bets.

Follow-up Actions

Documented segmentation methodology, mentored PMs on self-serve analysis, and handed off dashboards with regular refresh cadence to the product analytics team.
