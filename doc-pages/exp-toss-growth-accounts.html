Drove 2.1M+ new accounts (+30% growth in 6 months) by leading developers and designers to deploy viral referral, SEO optimization, and rewards programs, establishing Toss Bank as Korea’s fastest‑growing bank

Background

Growth Squad owned top-of-funnel acquisition during Toss Bank's scale-up year; leadership set an aggressive goal to become Korea's fastest-growing bank ahead of regulatory capital milestones.

Problem

Existing acquisition relied on paid media with rising CAC; organic and viral loops were underdeveloped, and landing pages weren't optimized for the bank's evolving value proposition.

Solution

Led a cross-functional pod to overhaul referral mechanics, launch a reward calendar, ship SEO-focused product stories, and refresh onboarding flows to highlight instant approval and joint account perks.

Analytics

Established north-star metric of verified accounts, with supporting dashboards on invite conversion, landing page scroll depth, and reward ROI; ran incrementality tests vs. paid media holdouts.

Result

Delivered 2.1M incremental accounts (+30% growth in six months), captured the #1 app store finance ranking for eight consecutive weeks, and cut blended CAC by 18%.

Follow-up Actions

Documented loop playbooks, shipped localization for key referral surfaces, and transitioned ownership to lifecycle marketing with clear guardrails on incentive spend.
