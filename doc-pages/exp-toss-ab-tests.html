Background

The new-account funnel spanned marketing landing pages, eKYC, and funding; we inherited scattered analytics and inconsistent hypotheses from rapid early growth.

Problem

Completion rate stalled and attribution was noisy; no single team owned a unified experimentation backlog, making it hard to learn quickly or invest engineering time wisely.

Solution

Built an experimentation playbook combining funnel analytics, survey insights, and usability testing; established a weekly triage to scope A/B tests with design and engineering, covering copy, flows, and identity checks.

Analytics

Centralized metrics in Amplitude and BigQuery, defined guardrail KPIs (fraud, time-to-approve), and automated significance reports to ship learnings within 48 hours of each test.

Result

Ran 15+ high-impact experiments over two quarters, lifted funnel conversion by 15%, and reduced drop-offs during identity verification by 22% without increasing manual review workload.

Follow-up Actions

Transitioned the backlog to a permanent growth experimentation guild and documented learnings to accelerate future launches across lending and deposits funnels.
