I trained over 20 colleagues at my previous company, maintaining a weekly retention rate above 80%.

  • Raycast: Smarter Spotlight for Mac.
  • Monologue: Voice-based keyboard replacement.
  • Motion: Personal project & task management tool.
  • Reader: Readwise’s newsletter manager—I love newsletters!
  • Ghost: My blogging platform.
  • Zed: My go-to IDE—partly because I love Rust!
