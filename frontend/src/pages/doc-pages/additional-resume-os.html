Resume OS

Resume OS is the AI-first resume experience I built for product hiring teams. It merges the familiar one-page narrative with two interactive layers: clickable bullet points reveal deeper portfolio stories, and an integrated interview bot answers follow-up questions in real time.

Why it matters

  • Manual resume screens miss context — reviewers rarely have time to click through case studies.
  • AI interview prep tools typically sit outside the resume, forcing candidates to maintain multiple assets.
  • Recruiters still want the scan-ability of a tight one-pager, but with faster paths to evidence.

How it works

  • Interactive bullets: Each highlight opens a templated deep-dive built with the same content system that powers this resume.
  • Interview bot: A lightweight ChatKit agent pre-loaded with my project library, designed so recruiters can ask “how” questions without scheduling a call.
  • Analytics loop: Supabase captures which stories and questions get the most attention, informing what to emphasize in follow-up conversations.

Early impact

  • Cut time-to-context for recruiters by giving them an interactive brief instead of static PDFs.
  • In pilot interviews, 3 of 4 hiring managers used the bot to dig deeper before the live call.
  • Created a repeatable template other candidates can adapt for their own AI-assisted resumes.
